The public library here as a subscription to a bunch of databases, one of those is called reference USA and a person can search businesses by many criteria, one of them being by radius around a given address. Helped me zero in on businesses within walking distance to put in apps and see how many prospects are within x-radius of a camp site.

Such fallback sites gives someone … someplace to fall back to if for whatever reason a preferred urban camp can’t be used. Doesn’t leave a person with all eggs in 1 basket.
