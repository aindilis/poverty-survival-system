]]> By: Angel https://survivalrecipes.wordpress.com/2008/01/12/hygiene/#comment-1368 Mon, 03 Jun 2013 07:19:52 +0000 http://survivalrecipes.wordpress.com/2008/01/12/hygiene/#comment-1368 When I was in this predicament I did not look homeless. I didn’t tell people either. I was lucky to know someone who had a cement garage that locked from inside so I had shelter. Albeit not super warm, it was much safer than sleeping on the streets. During this time I would get up each day and walk to the closest hotel to where I stayed. I would do a sponge bath in the bathroom and even washed my hair in the sink. Afterwards, I used the hand dryer as a hair dryer to dry my hair. I didn’t make much of a mess and always made sure it was clean before I left. In this way I was presentable and able to land a job.

There are usually places like shelters, YWCA’s, and the like that hand out free toothbrushes, brushes and other hygiene items as well.
